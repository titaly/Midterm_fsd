Virtual Companies

KITians render their internship by putting up Virtual Companies that cater to real clients with real life projects. These projects provide the necessary hands on experience our students need to become well versed in applying theoretical knowledge into industry practice.

These Virtual Companies aim to bridge the gap between the academe and the industry. KITians take on the different roles in the corporate world. They develop all the necessary skills to become future entrepreneurs.

KIT strives to assist start-ups. A successful Virtual Company has the opportunity to be transformed into a real corporation. The unique KIT internship model is what sets a KITian apart from the rest.
